DASHBUD!!!

Dashbud is a desktop pet that turns your daily tasks into something fun and motivating. Whether you’re writing an essay, studying, or trying to stay focused, your pet grows as you make progress. The more you work, the more your pet thrives! Unlocking activities like baking, crafting, building, and decorating your pet’s home! ✧˖°
