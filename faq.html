Is Dashbud free?

Yes! Basic features are free, with optional premium upgrades to unlock even more exclusive items and activities for your pet.

Does it track my data?

We only track productivity data (like word counts or focus time) to improve your experience and help your pet grow. Your privacy is our priority!

Can I customize my pet?

Absolutely! As you complete goals, you'll unlock different outfits, colors, and accessories to make your buddy truly unique.

What happens if I miss my daily goals?

Don't worry, your pet won't disappear! However, their energy might drop, and you might miss out on daily streak rewards. Just complete a quick task to cheer them up again.

How does Dashbud help me focus?

Dashbud uses a "focus timer" and task tracking. Your pet reacts to your activity: when you're working, your pet stays active and happy, but if you get distracted, your pet might get sleepy or nudges you to get back on track!

How do I contact support?

You can reach out to us through the Contact page or connect with us on Instagram and Twitter for updates and quick help!
